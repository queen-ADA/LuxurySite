The Chanel Story

Why We Started

Chanel (/ʃəˈnɛl/, French pronunciation: [ʃanɛl]) is a French luxury fashion house that was founded by couturière Coco Chanel in 1910. It focuses on women's high fashion and ready-to-wear clothes, luxury goods and accessories.The company is currently owned by Alain Wertheimer and Gérard Wertheimer, grandsons of Pierre Wertheimer, who was an early business partner of Coco Chanel.

Our Mission

"To be the Ultimate House of Luxury, defining style and creating desire, now and forever."
